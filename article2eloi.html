Hi everyone,
Today, I will explain you one of the best private part in Erasmus : The Food. When you are a Student in France, maybe you are sleeping always at your parent's home and you can't choose the food or quantities. But when you are in Erasmus you must control your food if not you'll die.

I can show you a recipe that I made : a half yam, 2 eggs and 100g of beef. You set your furnace on 200°C, on a pan you putt 10g of salt butter and a little bit of paprika. Then you break the eggs in the pan and mix it. When the eggs is done you put in furnace the yam that you have slice in the middle, after you have put oil on it with aromatic.

Then you put the meat in the pan, add salt, pepper and the spice of your choice. When the meat is cooked, you put the eggs back in the pan and lower the heat to a minimum. And you wait the yam, to see when it’s done you will see the square on it.
And after, have a nice meal.

Yam with eggs and beef

That is a good recipe because you can work during the cooking of the yam. But you need an other recipe if you don’t have time.
