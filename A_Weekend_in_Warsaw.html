A weekend in Warsaw
  • Romain J.
  • 3rd March

My friend Eloi and my-self must stop in Warsaw before arriving in Łódź because of our flight, who did not reach Łódź directly. So, we had booked an Airbnb flat for 2 people in the center of the Polish capital for 2 nights.

At our arrival at the airport, we planned to join our flat by train. We chose train because we thought it was cheaper than taxi and easier than buses because our luggage. The problem was train to Centralna train station was cancelled and we did not understand why at this moment. Finally, we decided to take a taxi. The taxi said our destination was difficult to reach because presidents Vladimir Zelenski and Joe Biden were on a meeting in Warsaw! It means that the city enter was blocked by a lot of policemen... 15 minutes later we arrived at our Airbnb flat with all our luggage.

In the evening we had explored the neighbourhood and visited a church before try to find a nice place to eat some typical food from Poland. We funded Hala Koszyki, it is a cool place with several restaurant with only street food but from all over the world.

The coolest thing is that you can order a meal and sit with other people who ordered in another restaurant than yours. I ordered polish sausage and pickles with fries and my friend tried fried pork meat with potatoes and polish pickles. We also wanted to taste polish beer.

After a good night we visited Warsaw city center : we went to the Science and Culture Palace to take picture of the view on the terrace, I was a little bit disappointed because the pictures looked like as all big cities in France, but we were happy to have done it.

Then, we wanted to see polish shops and mall to compare them to the French ones before eating another typical polish meal: the pierogies. There are raviolis filled by different foods such as meat or vegetables, cooked fish etc... and we enjoyed eating them.

During the last part of the day, we only walked in the old town because the Royal castle was closed due to the meeting between Vladimir Zelenski and Joe Biden. We also saw some journalist at the presidential palace who were waiting for them with their cameras and mike for many information channels.
